INTRODUCTION TO SMARTPARKING PARKING MANAGEMENT SYSTEM


Smartparking smart parking management system uses magnetic card access control technology for transportation systems, cars, motorbikes, using technological devices to replace human power. Brings convenience, safety and accuracy in parking lot security management.


The system allows for automatic and faster ticket recording and access control. Information recorded includes license plate numbers, vehicle card numbers, vehicle tickets, vehicle photos, vehicle photos, parking lot photos, etc. At the same time, using the software system helps managers operate the parking lot more easily. Reports on the number of vehicles entering and exiting, revenue control for the parking lot, and many other utilities.


Working principle


Vehicles entering the parking lot will be issued a magnetic card (also known as RFID) that has been registered in the system. When swiping the card, the Barrier will open, the camera will capture the license plate and analyze it into numbers. The card number and license plate will be stored simultaneously with the image of the driver taken by the second camera for query when necessary. The time the vehicle enters is calculated when the card is swiped and is the basis for calculating the parking time.

Entrance : The guard will take a card, swipe it with the card reader and give it back to the guest. The system will then take a photo of the license plate and the exterior. The license plate is recognized in text form combined with card parameters, entry and exit date and time, and vehicle driver to create a database stored on the access management system.

Exit : the staff will collect the customer's card or the customer will use his/her card to swipe it on the card reader. The system will take a photo of the license plate and the whole scene to identify the license plate. The software will automatically compare it with the license plate when entering and exiting to check if the parameters are valid or not. If the number is incorrect, the system will automatically send a warning signal to the staff. If correct, the software will display the payment amount.

Advantages of smart parking solutions compared to traditional parking methods


Traditional parking has many disadvantages:

  • – Staff manually records parking information. Use paper or write directly on the vehicle for control. This traditional way of parking has many disadvantages in terms of time and safety.
  • – Causes traffic congestion, especially during peak periods.
  • – Not very secure as tickets can be easily counterfeited, resulting in vehicle theft.
  • – Difficult to control revenue and a lot of data because it is only recorded manually.
  • – Traditional parking methods are only used in small motorbike parking lots and are not suitable for new, modern urban areas.

Smart parking solution with magnetic cards has many advantages:

  • – Regarding performance: using many modern devices helps control more closely and quickly .
  • – Automation capability: Using a magnetic card with a unique code that is difficult to counterfeit , the image of the license plate and the vehicle owner are stored in the system as soon as the driver swipes the card. Therefore, the security is higher than traditional methods, and the ability to retrieve the vehicle is also faster .
  • – The magnetic card vehicle management system is installed in large-scale parking lots such as buildings, apartments, shopping centers, etc. It helps parking lot managers save manpower because the operating mechanism on the system is the main one and the system implementation is also quite simple for the operator.
  • – Smart parking solutions, in addition to security control, also help investors manage revenue and business efficiency of parking lots during certain periods of time.
  • – Maintenance costs, warranty policy: the ability to install and use specialized equipment for smart parking systems has a fairly long usage time . Normally, the company that installs this smart system will closely monitor and have a separate maintenance policy for each parking lot.
  • – Smart parking solution with high professionalism and convenience for users .

Disadvantages of the solution

Besides the outstanding features, the smart parking solution using magnetic cards also has certain limitations that have not been resolved.

– Ability to find a vacant parking space: in hidden parking lots, it is more difficult for drivers to find a parking space, thus wasting time.

– For basement car parks, it is necessary to combine smarter equipment.

– Using magnetic cards can still be counterfeited if the system software is hacked.


BASIC EQUIPMENT OF SMART PARKING SYSTEM


A complete smart parking system will include full camera control equipment, card readers, cabins, computer systems, automatic barriers, sensors, etc. However, depending on the scale of the parking lot, certain equipment can be replaced to save costs for the investor. But in general, the system needs to ensure the following basic equipment:

System cabinet, security lock

  • – With smart parking management solutions using magnetic cards and barcodes, the first device to consider is the equipment cabinet (also known as a cabin or security booth). The cabinet is designed in many different forms to suit each parking location.
  • – Main function: contains computer and software system for parking management. Without computer and system, the solution cannot work.
  • – The computer and server systems will be consulted and implemented by the installation department to suit each parking lot model.

Parking lot surveillance camera

  • – Smart parking lot camera system includes license plate camera and panoramic camera.
  • – License plate camera type: specializes in reading and recording license plate parameters, so it is required in the smart parking management system. It can clearly see license plates in different lighting conditions.
  • – Panoramic camera type: control of drivers, overall entry and exit of the parking lot gate area. Depending on the size of the parking lot, it may or may not be necessary.

Card and Card Reader (Magnetic card parking machine)

  • – Function: like a regular ticket, the magnetic card helps control access to the parking lot.
  • – Card readers are divided into 2 types: fixed card readers (usually used for motorbikes and motorcycles) and remote card readers (used for cars).
  • – How it works: Depending on each smart parking model, the appropriate number of card readers will be used to bring efficiency to the parking management system.

>>Details of the models are in the content below the article.

  • – 1 machine 1 lane model uses 1 card reader
  • – Model of 1 computer managing 2 independent lanes uses 2 card readers
  • – Model of 1 computer managing 2 lanes for cars and motorbikes, use 1 card reader for all types of vehicles or use separate card readers for each type of vehicle.
  • – Model of 1 computer managing 3 or 4 lanes, use 2 card readers or use separate card readers for each type of vehicle.

Barrier gate (barrier bar)

  • Function: Barrier acts as an effective barrier gate for smart parking systems . This device helps reduce the speed of vehicles entering and exiting the parking lot to control the security and order of the parking lot.
  • Depending on the size of the parking lot, this device will be used. Because with small parking lots of less than 200 cars, it is not necessary to use barrier equipment to save costs for the investor. But with large parking lots with high traffic volume, this device is needed to provide a complete solution.
  • How it works: The barrier is installed right in front of the parking lot entrance/exit gate. When the vehicle owner swipes the card, the barrier will automatically open and allow the vehicle to move in/out.

Safety sensor

  • – Device function: usually connected to barrier devices to bring effective management to smart parking lots. Similar to barrier devices, safety sensors may not need to be used for small-scale parking lots or at the request of the investor. But basically, the parking system should use this device to ensure effective management solutions.

>>Smartparking management system uses sensors to ensure safety for vehicles entering and exiting.


Led Board

  • – Function: The LED panel helps display information about license plates or payment amounts when vehicles enter or exit, or can display instructions in standby mode.
  • – How it works: Connect to computer via COM port or TCP/IP

MODELS OF USING SMART PARKING MANAGEMENT SYSTEM


The smart parking lot model is divided into different lanes. Using the management system for each lane makes it easy to control vehicles entering and exiting depending on the size, terrain, and type of vehicle entering and exiting the parking lot.

Parking lots have separate entrance and exit gates (or multiple gates), each gate will use a separate computer for management and between these systems, these computers need to be connected to a LAN network to operate together.

System data can be saved on 1 computer in any lane, however, to increase system stability and ensure the most basic model of the smart parking solution, it is recommended to use 01 separate PC (or Server) as the Data Storage Server.

Each parking lot will have different terrain and location characteristics, so the design will also correspond to each different area. Normally, each computer will manage 1 lane, but there are cases where 1 computer can manage 2, 3, 4 different lanes.
