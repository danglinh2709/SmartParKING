Vehicle access control solutions

January 2, 2024

When it comes to smart parking systems, you will definitely think of a professional vehicle access control system extracted by magnetic cards. That's right, this is a vehicle management model that is widely replicated and highly valued because of the value and efficiency it brings. We are committed to safety and the use of modern control equipment when using the Megaparking management system.


Overview of smart vehicle access control system solution using magnetic cards and cameras
Smart vehicle access control system uses magnetic card technology combined with specialized surveillance cameras. The solution combines many devices such as camera systems, cabins, software, automatic barriers. The devices are installed and operated according to a certain principle.

Operating principle of vehicle entry and exit control solution
Vehicles entering and exiting the control area will be issued a magnetic card that has been registered in the system. When swiping the card, the barrier device automatically opens and the license plate camera records the information and analyzes it into data. The card number and license plate will be stored simultaneously with the driver's image taken by the second camera for query when necessary. The time the vehicle enters is calculated when the card is swiped and is the basis for calculating parking time.

Entrance: The guard will take a card, swipe it on the card reader and give it to the guest. The system will then take a photo of the license plate and the exterior. The license plate is recognized as text combined with card parameters, entry and exit date and time, and the driver to create a database stored on the entry and exit management system. Exit : The staff will collect the guest's card or the guest will use his own card to swipe it on the card reader. The system will take a photo of the license plate and the entire scene to identify the license plate. The software automatically compares it with the license plate at the time of entry and exit to check if the parameters are valid. If the number is incorrect, the system will automatically send a warning signal to the staff. If correct, the software will display the payment amount.


Solution advantages
• – Control vehicles entering and exiting completely and systematically. Ensure safety and modern convenience for vehicles entering and exiting buildings.
• – Manage fees and revenue accurately
• – Save manpower
• – Save time for customers entering and exiting. Reduce congestion or confusion, theft compared to previous traditional vehicle management systems.
• – Extremely fast operation when it only takes less than 2-3 seconds to get or park a vehicle
• – Stable management system, easy to operate
• – Controlling access by magnetic cards will replace the previous easily damaged paper cards. Magnetic cards are made of high-quality plastic, with prices ranging from 15,000 VND to 18,000 VND.
