Smart vehicle lifting solutions

January 2, 2024

The demand for cars in big cities and new urban areas is increasing. The system of outdoor parking lots and underground parking lots is also becoming overloaded. Therefore, finding smart parking solutions is getting more and more attention.

For shared parking lots for motorbikes, scooters and cars in shopping malls, buildings, apartments, hospitals, schools, etc., it is usually possible to use a vehicle management solution using magnetic cards or an automatic system solution to find vacant spaces. However, for high-end, specialized parking lots for cars, a more modern and automatic system will be needed.


There are many smart parking management solutions on the market, one of which is the automatic lifting parking solution. This solution is highly effective for multi-storey parking lots, large-scale areas with a capacity of 100-500 cars, high-end apartment buildings and commercial centers. The automatic lifting system uses many technological devices, so the investment level is also higher than conventional solutions, but in terms of long-term profits and utilities, it is a management system that brings high revenue to those who do business in this type of high-end parking lot. To understand what the smart parking management solution using an automatic lifting system (also known as a lift system) is like, how it is applied and what sizes of parking lots it can be applied to. Megatech would like to share details about the solution in the content below.


What is an automatic lift parking system?
“An automatic lift parking system is a system that uses an elevator to lift cars up and down between floors. This elevator has the task of both picking up the car at the parking spot and moving along the parking floors.”

The parking management solution using a lifting system (also known as an automatic parking system) is one of the smart parking management solutions that helps manage parking lots effectively. The mechanism of the solution is to use a lifting bar to move vehicles into the parking lot. The lifting bar system is a type of elevator that lifts cars up and down between floors; this lifting bar is used to retrieve cars on the spot and simultaneously lift and lower cars along the parking floors.
The solution is a series of automatic parking systems, integrating many technological devices into a common system, which is the car lift. This solution helps save investment costs, reduce parking and retrieving time, and is easier to use when it is integrated into a multifunctional device.


Advantages of automatic lifting parking management solution
Automatic lifting parking is a solution that applies many modern technological devices. Therefore, it brings high efficiency to both managers and users.
• – Suitable for new urban areas; high-end commercial centers; building systems that need to save parking space.
• –Automatic parking system is suitable for many parking lots with a capacity of 100-500 cars.
• – The system has the ability to control simply with a touch screen when vehicles enter and exit the parking lot.
• – Automatic lifting parking solution can be installed underground or above ground. Assembled according to location, scale and customer needs.
• – The solution is increasingly researched and improved with smarter, more convenient devices.


Disadvantages of the solution
Besides the outstanding advantages, the system still has certain disadvantages.
• – The solution mainly uses the elevator system and they have to operate continuously. So it is easy to encounter problems and needs regular maintenance, so it costs a lot for the investor.
• – Using a robot systemto get cars in and out of the parking lot, so the cost is quite high.
• – The parking floor of the system uses a concrete floor with high requirements for technology and accuracy of the ground. Therefore, it is difficult to construct with a slower construction time than the smart parking solution using magnetic cards.
• – The process of retrieving cars of the moving elevator system is a bit long because it cannot be deposited and retrieved at the same time.
