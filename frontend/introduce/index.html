About SmartParking

LTT Corporation is a leading company in developing smart parking management software and operating parking facilities across Vietnam. We offer comprehensive solutions combining advanced technology with efficient project management to optimize parking operations and enhance urban mobility. With an experienced team and commitment to innovation, LTT pioneers smart transportation solutions and supports the sustainable development of smart cities nationwide.

SmartParking of LTT is an intelligent parking system designed to improve efficiency and user experience in urban environments. Featuring a user-friendly interface and stable performance, it integrates automated payment for cars and motorbikes, replacing manual processes for fast, accurate parking management. It also includes software for vehicle tracking, revenue reporting, and streamlined operations.

LTT is also a professional contractor in parking lot construction and management. We provide modern parking solutions, integrating advanced technology to optimize space and ensure efficient management. With expertise in managing multiple parking lots, we deliver tailored solutions to meet customer needs, ensuring flexibility and smooth operations throughout.

Disadvantages of Traditional Parking Management

  • ✔
    The staff records the parking information manually. Use paper or write directly on the vehicle to control. Such a traditional way of parking has many inadequacies in terms of time and safety.
  • ✔
    Causing traffic jams, especially during peak periods.
  • ✔
    High safety is not ensured when bus tickets are easily counterfeited, car theft occurs.
  • ✔
    It is difficult to control revenue and a lot of data because it is only written in manual form.
  • ✔
    The traditional way of parking is only used in small motorbike parks, which is not suitable for new and modern urban areas.

Advantages of Smart Parking Management System

  • ✔
    The number plate recognition AI technology allows for a high accuracy of over 95%.
  • ✔
    The system adopts a fully automatic feature at both the entrance and exit gates when there is no need for direct intervention protection. Tickets are automatically paid via dynamic Qrcode.
  • ✔
    Monthly ticket car: Automatic entry and exit, with App management. Face recognition can be applied.
  • ✔
    Turn-in tickets: Automatic card issuance in the entry direction and automatic payment in the exit lane.
  • ✔
    When the connection to the server is lost. The machines will still work normally and be used as a temporary server, when there is a reconnection, the data will be automatically synchronized.
