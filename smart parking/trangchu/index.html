SmartParking Management System

SmartParking professional software along with modern system equipment helps managers control and accurately count the number of vehicles entering and exiting at all times through license plate recognition cameras, vehicle owners' faces, and magnetic cards. Issuing and collecting cards at the entrance gate becomes easy, civilized, and effective. This not only helps save time for vehicle owners when parking at the parking lot but also helps avoid congestion and creates a professional space for apartment buildings, hospitals, schools, and factories.

The components of Smart Parking Systems®

Sensor

Buried under every kind of road surface, precisely set in order to give accurate information in real time about parking lot status free or busy.

Cameras

Placed on a lamppost or an high pointview, cameras for the smart parking detection activities are a technological evolution developed to face the new urban necessities.

Mobile App

4Park allows to manage payments and virtualized smart cards. It drives the user to the selected parking lot and allows to pay or to activate and end the parking stop.

Central server

Where the data are collected. Communicating in realtime with sensors or Mobile APP are sent, through a radio communication network that uses alternative communication protocols or co-present.

Management software

Processes the data received in real time and makes them available through the Internet network, on any type of user terminal (PC, notebook, smartphone, tablet, PDA) for use and consultation by stakeholders.

Barrier

Support staff to control or automatically open/close (after the system extracts and checks correct information) to ensure safety and security in the parking lot.
