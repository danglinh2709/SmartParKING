Parking guidance solution

April 1, 2025

Smart parking solution with automatic vacancy notification system is one of the parking solutions at Smartparking. Effective solution for car parking lots. The solution is designed to combine access control technology with magnetic cards and location notification system in the parking lot. With a professional and modern management system; suitable solution for car parking models in the basement of buildings and apartments.

Currently, with conventional parking lots for cars, it takes a lot of time to find a parking space, or there is a situation where cars enter the lot but there is no vacancy. This situation is time-consuming and annoying for vehicles as well as parking lot managers. Based on this situation, we have designed and built a smart parking model with an automatic vacancy notification system. The solution combines many smart and systematic control devices. Bringing high efficiency to entry and exit activities in the parking lot.


OVERVIEW OF AUTOMATIC PARKING SOLUTION TO FIND VACANT SPACES

The system uses magnetic card access control technology combined with a vacant space notification device and smart parking guidance. With the function of the system reporting the status of whether the parking lot has a space or not, to avoid wasting time when entering and exiting the parking lot and parking quickly.
The automatic parking lot solution finds a location, also known as the parking guidance system, the basement vacancy notification system. This is a solution applied to the basement of many current building, apartment and office parking lots.
This system is fully automatic, uses sensor technology for each location and allows for accurate notification of the number of vacant spaces in each specific area and basement. At the same time, it guides the driver to find a parking space as quickly as possible through sensors installed at each parking location.

Working principle

When the driver enters the checkpoint, the driver will visually observe information about the parking status, which spaces are available, displayed on the electronic board located right at the checkpoint. Here, based on this display, the operator can advise and guide the driver to park the vehicle in the most convenient space.


Operating principle of automatic parking system

In case the driver does not pay attention or does not have the overall information about the number of vacant parking spaces in the parking areas, the driver will be able to observe the information displayed on the LED board about directions, the indicator lights about the status of the parking spots with or without cars in the current area to quickly and easily determine a reasonable parking location.

Details of the basement vacancy notification system

The automatic parking solution basically operates based on the principle of access control using RFID technology, improved with other devices to improve the efficiency of the parking lot for both supervisors and customers. The automatic smart parking management system includes access control devices and parking guidance systems.

Access control devices

These are devices installed and operated at the parking lot control station. Similar to the basic magnetic card parking solution. This control station will include devices connected together according to a certain process to ensure the operating principle and stable operation.

Parking Guidance System

The parking system is divided into separate parking spots. At each spot, a sensor will be installed, this sensor helps determine the current status of that parking spot (with or without a car). Along with that is an LED device that displays the status (a green light indicates that the spot is empty, and a red light indicates that the spot is occupied). Those sensors will be connected to the zone controller (ZCU)
