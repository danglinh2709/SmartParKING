Currently, there are many smart parking solutions on the market, one of which is the professional apartment building parking management system from Smartparking. Overview of smart parking management system

Smart parking management system uses magnetic card access control technology. The system allows for automatic and faster ticket recording and access control. Information recorded includes license plate number, card number, ticket, car photo, car photo, parking attendant photo, etc. At the same time, using a software system helps managers operate the parking lot more easily. Reports on the number of vehicles entering and exiting, revenue control for the parking lot, and many other utilities.

Equipment of apartment building vehicle management system

Operating principle of apartment parking lot access control solution

Vehicles entering and exiting the apartment parking lot will be issued a FIRD card registered in the management software system. When swiping the card, the barrier will open and the camera will record information about the license plate, the whole scene of people and vehicles moving into the parking lot. The vehicle management system in and out of the apartment includes managing vehicles entering and exiting the apartment residents and visitors for easy control.

Operating principle of smart parking solution using magnetic card

For those living and working in the apartment building

  • - Each person will register in advance with the parking lot manager to be issued a fixed parking card;
  • - The recorded information includes name, vehicle type, license plate number... to be updated in the parking lot management software system.
  • - When entering and exiting the parking lot, it is necessary to swipe the registered cards and the system will confirm the entry and exit.
  • - Normally, the parking fee will be paid monthly.

For visitors

  • - Vehicle entry and exit management is done by: when the person and vehicle enter the apartment parking area. The staff uses the card and swipes the card through the card reader. At this time, the system will collect information about the date and time of entry and the vehicle license plate number for control.
  • - When the information is collected, the barrier system automatically opens. Vehicles will automatically enter and exit the area.
  • - When the vehicle exits, the staff collects the card and proceeds to pay.

For car parks in new urban areas, a smart system is needed. In addition to controlling access by magnetic cards, there is also a system to notify vacant spaces and guide smart parking. The solution is highly effective, when only need to install additional position sensors and LED board systems. The solution is implemented in the following order:

Apartment car park management solution

First step: with the arrangement of LED signs right outside the entrance gate. When the car arrives at the parking garage, the vehicle owner will know if there is a vacant parking space. This is known through the signal parameters on the LED sign; the sign is always updated thanks to the installed system.

Next step: when entering the garage, the vehicle swipes the card normally and arrives at the parking lot. The sensor system is installed at each parking location. If there is no car, the light will signal green, and if there is a car parked, the light will signal red. The solution brings convenience when you do not have to go around looking for a parking spot or ask the staff.
