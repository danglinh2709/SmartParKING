Installing shopping center parking management system

Step 1: Select the model and operate the computer system Usually, the shopping mall parking management system uses a model of 2 or more computers. Each computer will manage separate entry and exit lanes.

Step 2: Select separate entry and exit lanes for the shopping mall parking system Use at least 1 separate exit lane and 1 separate entry lane for the parking lot.

Incoming lane:
  • – Customers enter the inbound lane and stop at the card swiping position. Let the camera take a picture and record the license plate number.
  • – The security guard just needs to press Enter to confirm that there is a vehicle. Let the computer receive data from the camera and then transfer the data to the card scanner.
  • – The staff inserts the card into the card scanner to integrate the data on the smart magnetic card chip. This is like each magnetic card is a customer ID. Of course, that is also the only ID code that the customer has.
  • – The entire operation takes about 03 seconds. It does not waste effort, does not take too much time but is highly effective.
Outgoing lane:
  • – The customer hands over the magnetic card to the staff to scan the card into the card reader. Let the computer compare it with the original license plate number.
  • – When there is no discrepancy and the information is valid, the barrier automatically opens for the customer.
  • – This operation takes about 02 -> 03 seconds.

Shopping center parking system management

2, Automatic parking management solution for shopping centers with basement parking systems.

In addition to the outdoor parking lots of shopping malls, there are now a large number of parking systems using basements to manage the entry and exit of vehicles, especially in new urban areas. Especially commercial buildings integrate many systems. With the characteristic of a fairly large parking basement, in addition to the card swipe control system. The system is also integrated with sensors that indicate vacant spaces and automatic parking guidance.

Management of parking system in shopping mall

The system is applied to high-end shopping malls with underground parking lots for cars. The solution combines access control by magnetic card and LED board system, alarm sensor. When the driver brings the car to the control point, the driver will visually observe information about the parking status, the vacant space is shown on the electronic board located right at the control point. Here, based on this display, the operator can advise and guide the driver to put the vehicle in the most convenient vacant space. The solution of reporting vacant space and parking guidance system helps save human resources, speed up smart parking progress. The solution is specialized for car parking lots in the basement, suitable for new urban areas.
