Car and motorbike shared parking model

Key Characteristics

  • Equipment: Manage a variety of different equipment brands. Allows easy and flexible upgrading, replacement, etc.
  • Friendly interface, easy to use, manage multiple lanes, allow lane switching
  • Monthly tickets paid monthly, quarterly, yearly. Automatic payment of single-trip tickets via dynamic Qrcode, or cash payment
  • Vehicle management: Monthly car tickets, single-trip car tickets, Monthly motorbike tickets, single-trip motorbike tickets, Bicycles
  • Card management: VIP, monthly subscription, single-trip tickets with diverse calculation formulas, easy to change unit prices
  • Exception management: All cases of forced barrier opening are recorded in history, with images saved
  • Report lost cards, warn of vehicles in Black lists, etc.
  • Report the number of vehicles and strictly control vehicles entering and exiting
  • Transparent and clear toll collection reports
  • Security of vehicle tickets, cannot be counterfeited
  • Authorities for security guards, specialized staff, and managers
  • 24/7 support when the system encounters difficulties or problems

Key System Requirements

  • Computer with SmartParking software
  • Camera, power source, card and card reader
  • Network switch, UPS, Fence post set
  • Barrier, Loop Detector, central working cabinet
  • Other installation materials
